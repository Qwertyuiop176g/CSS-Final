Top 5 Attractions to Visit

Singapore is a vibrant city that blends modern landmarks, rich cultural heritage, and world-class wildlife. Visitors can enjoy stunning architecture, bustling markets, delicious local food, and unique experiences that make the city a must-visit destination.

Whether you’re interested in sightseeing, exploring local culture, or enjoying nature, Singapore has something for everyone. Here, we highlight the top 5 attractions you shouldn’t miss during your visit.

Marina Bay Sands

Address: 10 Bayfront Ave, Singapore 018956
Official website: www.marinabaysands.com

Marina Bay Sands is one of Singapore’s most iconic landmarks, renowned for its luxury, architectural brilliance, and breathtaking skyline views.

From elegant indoor venues to the famous rooftop infinity pool, the destination offers unforgettable experiences for visitors and events alike.

Singapore Flyer

Address: 30 Raffles Ave, Singapore 039803
Official website: www.singaporeflyer.com

Standing at 165 metres, the Singapore Flyer offers stunning 360-degree views of Marina Bay, the Singapore River, and the city skyline.

Visitors can enjoy unique experiences such as sky dining, cocktail capsules, and private event spaces.

Chinatown

Address: Chinatown district, Singapore
Visitor info: chinatownsg.com

Chinatown is a lively historic neighbourhood filled with colourful shophouses, markets, and temples such as the Buddha Tooth Relic Temple.

It is also famous for local street food and is best explored on foot.

Singapore Zoo

Address: 80 Mandai Lake Rd, Singapore 729826
Official website: mandai.com/en/singapore-zoo.html

Known for its open-concept habitats, the Singapore Zoo allows animals to roam in environments similar to their natural homes.

Interactive shows and feeding sessions make it ideal for families and students.

Jewel Changi Airport

Address: 60 Airport Blvd, Singapore 819643
Official website: changiairport.com

Jewel Changi Airport combines nature, shopping, and architecture, featuring the iconic Rain Vortex and indoor gardens.

It is a must-visit destination even if you are not flying.
