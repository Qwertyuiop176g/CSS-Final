Top 5 attractions to visit

Singapore is a vibrant city that blends modern landmarks, rich cultural heritage, and world-class wildlife. Visitors can enjoy stunning architecture, bustling markets, delicious local food, and unique experiences that make the city a must-visit destination.

Whether you’re interested in sightseeing, exploring local culture, or enjoying nature, Singapore has something for everyone. Here, we highlight the top 5 attractions you shouldn’t miss during your visit.

Marina Bay Sands

Address: 10 Bayfront Ave, Singapore 018956
Official website: www.marinabaysands.com

Impress your guests with an extraordinary event at Marina Bay Sands, an iconic destination renowned for its world-class venues and breathtaking skyline views. As one of Singapore’s most prestigious landmarks, Marina Bay Sands offers a seamless blend of luxury, innovation, and architectural brilliance, creating an unforgettable setting for any occasion.

Host an elegant indoor affair within sophisticated event spaces overlooking the vibrant Marina Bay, or elevate your celebration with a stunning rooftop experience above the city lights. From grand ballrooms to exclusive sky-high venues, each space is designed to deliver a refined and memorable atmosphere.

Singapore Flyer

Address: 30 Raffles Ave, Singapore 039803
Official website: www.singaporeflyer.com

At a height of 165 metres, the Singapore Flyer is one of the world's largest observation wheels. Located in the heart of the city, this tourist attraction offers stunning 360-degree views of the island’s iconic and historical landmarks – from Marina Bay to the Singapore River, Raffles Place, Merlion Park and the Padang.

The Singapore Flyer offers a variety of corporate packages such as the World's First Full Butler Sky Dining and VIP Signature Cocktail private capsules. Indulge in the famous Singapore Sling as you and your guests soar high above with an unrivalled view of the city's skyline.

Use the Flyer's event spaces like the Flyer Lounge or Flyer Event Hall for your next meeting, and the 28 Flyer capsules as break out rooms. Each capsule has a capacity of 28 pax.

China town

Address: Chinatown district, Singapore
Visitor info site: chinatownsg.com

Chinatown is a lively historic neighbourhood with colourful shophouses, markets, and temples. Visitors can stroll along Pagoda Street and Temple Street for souvenirs and local snacks. Key landmarks include the Buddha Tooth Relic Temple and Sri Mariamman Temple, highlighting the area’s rich cultural heritage.

Chinatown is a great place to try local food at Chinatown Complex Food Centre, where visitors can enjoy chicken rice, noodles, and traditional desserts. It is easily accessible via Chinatown MRT Station and is best explored on foot.

Singapore Zoo

Address: 80 Mandai Lake Rd, Singapore 729826
Official website: mandai.com/en/singapore-zoo.html

The Singapore Zoo is one of the world’s most famous wildlife parks, known for its open-concept, natural-style enclosures that allow animals to roam in environments similar to their natural habitats. Visitors can see a wide variety of animals, including tigers, orangutans, elephants, giraffes, and polar bears.

The zoo offers interactive experiences such as animal feeding sessions, shows, and guided tours, making it educational and fun for all ages. It is located in Mandai, easily accessible by bus or taxi, and is ideal for a full-day family outing.

Singapore Airport

Address: 60 Airport Blvd, Singapore 819643
Official website: changiairport.com

Create a truly memorable event at Jewel Changi Airport, a world-renowned lifestyle destination that seamlessly blends nature, architecture, and innovation. Home to the iconic Rain Vortex and lush indoor gardens, Jewel offers a unique setting where modern design meets tranquil greenery.

Whether you are planning an intimate celebration or a distinctive corporate gathering, Jewel Changi Airport offers an unforgettable atmosphere. With its stunning visuals, contemporary facilities, and unparalleled connectivity, your event is sure to captivate and inspire every guest.
