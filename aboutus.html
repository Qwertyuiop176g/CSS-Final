About This Website

Welcome!

This website was created to help tourists discover the best attractions in Singapore. As a beginner web developer, I wanted to build a simple and helpful guide for people planning their trip to Singapore.

Singapore is an amazing city with so much to see and do! I hope this website makes it easier for you to plan your visit and find fun places to explore.

About Singapore

Location: Southeast Asia (island country)

Languages: English, Mandarin, Malay, Tamil

Currency: Singapore Dollar (SGD)

Population: About 5.6 million people

Size: Very small! Only 734 square kilometers

Fun Fact: Singapore is both a city AND a country!

What Makes Singapore Special?

  • Very clean and safe
  • Mix of modern and traditional
  • Amazing food from many cultures
  • Efficient public transport
  • English widely spoken
  • Beautiful gardens and parks
  • Strict rules (but that keeps it safe and clean!)
