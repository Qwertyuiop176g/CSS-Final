Welcome to the Trip Planner

This trip planner helps you organise your journey by allowing you to choose destinations, plan routes, and manage your travel schedule easily.

How to Use the Trip Planner

  1. Select the number of days for your trip.
  2. Select the destinations you wish to visit.
  3. Press generate plan.
  4. Save or adjust your plan as needed.
